

# FREMI V/ FRODE FREMMERLID - Single Page Website

## Overview
A professional single-page website for a Norwegian machine contractor (maskinentreprenør) company based in Skodje, Møre og Romsdal. Clean, professional design with a construction/industrial feel.

## Design Direction
- Professional, trustworthy aesthetic with earthy/industrial tones (dark blues, oranges/yellows as accents)
- Clean typography, good whitespace
- Fully responsive for mobile and desktop
- Norwegian language throughout

## Sections (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Header
- **Top bar**: Contact info strip — email (fro-fre@outlook.com), phone (926 04 072), location (6260 Skodje), Org nr (912 166 805)
- **Navigation bar**: Logo/company name "FREMI" on left, 4 nav links on right (Forside, Tjenester, Om oss, Kontakt) that smooth-scroll to sections. Hamburger menu on mobile.

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with automatic sliding carousel (2 slides)
- **Slide 1**: "Pålitelig og sikker maskinentreprenør!" with subtitle about trust and references
- **Slide 2**: "VI SKAL VÆRE DITT NATURLIGE VALG..." text
- Each slide has a dark overlay background with a call-to-action button (e.g., "Kontakt oss")

### 3. Services Section (Tjenester)
- Section title: "Graving, drenering og transport av masser i Møre og Romsdal!"
- 4 service cards in a grid (2x2 on desktop, stacked on mobile):
  - **Graving** — excavation and groundwork
  - **Drenering** — drainage services
  - **Tomteutgraving** — site excavation
  - **Massetransport** — mass transport
- Each card with an icon, title, and description text

### 4. About Us Section (Om oss)
- "Welcome to Fremi!" heading with company description paragraph
- Call-to-action for non-binding chat

### 5. Why Choose Us Section
- 3 feature cards/columns:
  - "Let professionals do the job" — quality assurance
  - "At the agreed time and price" — reliability
  - "Flexible player" — adaptable solutions
- Each with an icon and short description

### 6. Contact Section (Kontakt)
- Two-column layout:
  - **Left**: Embedded map placeholder showing Skodje area
  - **Right**: Contact form with fields for name*, email*, phone, message*, consent checkbox, and submit button
- Contact details below: phone, address, org number

### 7. Footer
- Company info, quick links, contact details
- Copyright notice

### 8. Scroll-to-Top Button
- Fixed button in bottom-right corner that appears on scroll and smoothly scrolls back to top
